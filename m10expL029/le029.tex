	\noindent{\bf Task 7.}\\
	From 2016 to 2028 there are 12 years. The growth factor is
	$$a=(1-{p \over 100})= 1-0.0125 = 0.9875$$
	The pollutant emissions after 12 years are:
	$$y=100\% \cdot 0.9875^{12} =85.99\% $$
	So it has decreased by a little over 14%. The goal is achieved.
