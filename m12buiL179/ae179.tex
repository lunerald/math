	{\bf Bernoulli chains and binomial distribution}
	
	\begin{enumerate}[1.]
		{\bf \item An advertising campaign promises:} "There is a Star Wars figure in every seventh surprise egg". You open seven surprise eggs. What is the probability that you...
		\begin{enumerate}[a)]
			\item ...find exactly one Star Wars figure?
			\item ...can't find a Star Wars figure?
			\item ...find at least one Star Wars figure?
			\item ...only find a Star Wars figure in the seventh egg?
			\item ...find two Star Wars figures?
			\item ...find exactly two Star Wars figures and they follow one after the other?
		\end{enumerate}
		{\bf \item Two thirds of seniors in Germany own a cell phone.}
		Determine the probability of being selected randomly among 30
		Seniors at least 17 and a maximum of 23
		Own a cell phone.
