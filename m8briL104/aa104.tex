{\bf \item Gib die Definitionsmenge an und löse folgende Bruchgleichungen: }

\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{3}
\begin{tabular}{p{5 cm}p{5 cm}p{5 cm}}
a) $1 = {3 \over x-1}$ & b) $4  = { 2\over y-1 }$ &c) ${1 \over x} =1- { 2 \over x}$  \\
d) ${4x \over x+1} = 2$ & e) ${x \over x+1} = {1 \over 2}$ & f) ${2x \over x-1 } = {6 \over x-1}$ \\
g) ${x \over x^2 } = {5 \over 25}$  &h) ${x-5 \over x } = {-2 \over x }$& i) ${x-3 \over 2(x-3)} = x $\\ j) ${x \over 2x+8} = 1$ &k) ${2(x+1) \over x+1} = {1 \over 2}x$  &l) $\frac{1}{6x-2} = 1 $  
\end{tabular}

\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{3}
\begin{tabular}{p{5 cm}p{5 cm}p{5 cm}}
a) ${4 \over 2x} + {1 \over x} $	&b) ${x^2 \over x+1} - {2 \over 2(x+1) }$& c) ${1 \over x+1}- {1 \over x+1}$ \\ 
d) ${-10 \over 5x} + {2 \over x}$& e) ${x^2 +2x +1 \over x+1}$ &f) ${x^2 +x \over x}$ \\
