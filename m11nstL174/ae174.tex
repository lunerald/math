	{\bf Zeroing training sheet }\\
	
	{ \bf The following methods are required for this training sheet:}\
	
	\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{2}
	\begin{tabular}{lll}
		(BIN) & Exploiting a binomial formula & $x^2-36 =(x-6)(x+6)$\\
		(SOL) & midnight formula &
		$ax^2+bx+c=0 \leadsto x_{1/2}=\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$\\
		(VIE) & Vieta's theorem & $x^2+3x+2=(x+a)(x+b)$\\
		& & $a+b =3;~a\cdot b=2$\\
		(GUE) & guess if zeros are integer & $x^3 +2x^2 -x-2=0; ~ x_1=1$ \\
		(SUB) & substitution to reduce degrees & $x^4-4x^2+4=0;~u=x^2 \leadsto u^2-4u+4=0$\\
		(EXC) & exclude if possible & $ 2x^3 +x^2 = x^2(2x-1)$\\
		(POL) & polynomial division by known LSG & $ (x^3 +2x^2 -x-2):(x-1)=\dots$
	\end{tabular}
