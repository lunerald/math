	{\bf Functional mappings}
	\begin{enumerate}[1.]
		{\bf \item Examine which figure is described by the following equation:}
		$$ \left(
		\begin{array}{c}
			x'\\
			y'
		\end{array}
		\right) =
		\left(
		\begin{array}{cc}
			0.60 & -0.80 \\
			0.80 & 0.60
		\end{array}
		\right)
		\odot
		\left(
		\begin{array}{c}
			x\\
			y
		\end{array}
		\right)$$
		Specify the matrix in general and, if necessary, the displacement vector, the equation of the mirror axis, the center of rotation and the angle of rotation!
		
		%Exercise 2
		{\bf \item The (incomplete) matrix }
		$ M = \left(
		\begin{array}{cc}
			-0.71 & z \\
			0.71 & y
		\end{array}
		\right)$
		should be a mirror matrix that is assigned to an axis reflection on an origin line $g$. Determine $z$ and $y$ and by calculation the equation of the mirror axis $g$.
		
		%Task 3
		{\bf \item The line $h: y=2x+2$ is created by mirroring the axis on the line
			$s: y=-\frac{2}{3}x$ mapped to the line $h'$.} Use the parameter method to determine the equation of the line $h'$.
		
		{\bf \item The parabola $ p $ with the equation $ y = x^2 - 2x +4 $ is mapped to $ p' $ by reflection on the y-axis.}
		
		\begin{enumerate}[a)]
			\item Draw the illustration.
			\item Find the equation of the image parabola $ p' $ by calculation.
			\item Determine the inverse function $ f $ to $ p' $ using drawings and calculations.
		\end{enumerate}
		
	\end{enumerate}
