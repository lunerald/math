	{\bf powers with rational exponents.} For all tasks the following applies: $\mathbb{G}=\mathbb{R}^+$
	\begin{enumerate}[1.]
		
		{\bf \item A DIN A0 sheet} has an area of $A_{A0}= 1 \rm{m^2}. $ Length and width of this sheet behave like $\sqrt{2} : 1 $\\
		\begin{minipage}{0.5\textwidth}
			
			\begin{enumerate}[a)]
				\item How long and how wide is the A0 sheet? Write the solution exactly as a power of 2 and as a rounded decimal number.
				
				An A1 sheet is exactly half of the A0 sheet and has the same aspect ratio.
				By further dividing by two you get the formats A2, A3 and so on.
				
				\item Tabulate the lengths, widths and areas of the A0 to A4 sheets.
				Write the solution exactly as a power of 2 and as a rounded decimal number.
			\end{enumerate}
		\end{minipage}
