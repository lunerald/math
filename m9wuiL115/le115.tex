	\noindent{\bf Task 5.}\\
	The large square has side length $\sqrt{12,25}=3.5~{\rm LE}$; the small one has side length $\sqrt{6,25}=2.5~{\rm LE}$. The corners through which the line runs then have the coordinates $E_1(-3.5|3.5)$ and $E_2(-6|2.5)$.
	So the slope m is:
	$$ m = {3.5-2.5 \over -3.5+6}=0.4$$
	The y-intercept t is:
	$$3.5 = 0.4\cdot(-3.5) + t \quad \Rightarrow t = 4.9$$
	Functional equation:
	$$g: \quad y=0.4x+4.9$$
